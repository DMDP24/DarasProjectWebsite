Say Yes To The Dress Bridal store is here to help you look radiant on your special day. We stock stylish, elegant and desirable wedding dresses and accessories and have been operating in Kildare since 2010. Our company mainly deals with brides from Kildare, Laois and Wicklow, but we have had customers coming from all over the country to visit our beautiful salon. Our bridal store has 10 staff with five consultants working on the bridal shop floor dealing with our brides.

Our aim at Say Yes To The Dress Bridal is to provide a personalised, serene, graceful shopping experience for our brides, where they feel that they are their consultants number one priority and we will not rest until our bride is looking her best on her big day. We offer a range of dresses from every style of bride and wedding, classic, boohoo, glamourous and slinky but all of them showstopping.

We stock fabulous dresses from four top international wedding dress designers that are carried by Kleinfled Bridal in New York: Lazaro, Randy Fenoli, Pnina Tornai and Haley Paige. These fabulous dresses are available in sizes 8 to 26 and can be ordered in larger sizes if required.

Take a look at some of the dresses we have on offer from each designer to gain some ideas and inspiration for your big day, and when you are ready, head over to our booking form to schedule and appointment to find your dream dress.
