Cuban-born Lazaro was always drawing and painting as a child, realising the ideas for dresses that he had only existed in his imagination he attended the Ray College of Design in Chicago and started to make those visions a reality.

His natural talent, cultivated while helping his mother make the family’s clothing, was recognized in 1981 when he was awarded “Best New Bridal Designer” in a national competition. A master of the contemporary-classic look after almost a decade with his own label, Lazaro’s designs are timeless in their elegance.

A Lazaro bride can find any dress she can imagine within our collection from Lazaro we have everything from slender silhouettes to ball gowns and styles that range from simple to extravagant.
